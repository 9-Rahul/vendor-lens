# Internal Security Framework v2

## REQ 4.1.A - Data Residency
Vendor must ensure all PII is stored and processed exclusively within EU jurisdictions.

## REQ 4.1.B - Encryption at Rest
All stored data must be encrypted using AES-256 or stronger algorithms.

## REQ 4.2.A - Annual Penetration Testing
Vendor must conduct and provide results of annual third-party penetration testing.
